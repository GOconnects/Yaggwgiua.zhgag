The Lost Art of Letter Writing

Master timeless communication in the digital age with these professional techniques.

Essential Letter Formats

Business Letter

Structure: Your Address → Date → Recipient's Address → Salutation → Body → Closing → Signature

Example: "Dear Mr. Smith, I am writing to formally express my interest in..."

Personal Letter

Key Elements: Warm greeting → Personal updates → Questions for recipient → Closing sentiments

Example: "My dearest Eleanor, The spring blossoms here remind me of our walks through..."

Professional Techniques

  • The Three-Paragraph Rule: Introduction → Purpose → Action Item
  • Ink & Paper Selection: 100gsm ivory paper with navy/dark gray ink
  • Envelope Etiquette: Hand-written addresses with return wax seal

Condolence Letter

Formula: Acknowledge loss → Share memory (if applicable) → Offer support

Example: "Dear Margaret, I was deeply saddened to learn of John's passing. I'll always remember his..."

Modern Applications

Even in digital communication, letter-writing principles elevate your emails:

Subject: Follow-Up Regarding [Topic]

Body: "Dear Prof. Chen, Thank you for your time last Tuesday. As discussed, I'm attaching..."
