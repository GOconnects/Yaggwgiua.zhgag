Israeli-Palestinian Conflict: Full History & Atrocities

From Ancient Roots to 2024 – A Timeline of Oppression & Resistance

Israel the predator

Origins of the Conflict (2000 BCE – 1917)

2000 BCE: Canaanites & Early Settlements

Indigenous Canaanites inhabit the land. Hebrews (Israelites) later establish kingdoms.

70 CE: Roman Expulsion of Jews

After Jewish revolts, Romans destroy Jerusalem, rename the land "Syria Palaestina."

636 CE: Islamic Conquest

Muslims take control; Al-Aqsa Mosque built. Palestinians (Arabized descendants of Canaanites, Jews, and others) become majority.

1517–1917: Ottoman Rule

Palestine is multi-ethnic (Muslims, Christians, Jews under Ottoman Empire).

British Mandate & Zionist Colonization (1917–1948)

1917: Balfour Declaration

Britain pledges support for a "Jewish homeland" in Palestine, ignoring indigenous Palestinians.

1936–1939: Arab Revolt

Palestinians resist British-Zionist colonization; massacres like Deir Yassin (1948) foreshadow ethnic cleansing.

Nakba & Israeli Statehood (1948–1967)

1948: Nakba ("Catastrophe")

Zionist militias expel 750,000+ Palestinians, destroy 500+ villages. Israel established on 78% of Palestine.

1964: PLO Founded

Palestinians organize armed resistance under Yasser Arafat.

Occupation & Resistance (1967–2024)

1967: Six-Day War

Israel occupies West Bank, Gaza, East Jerusalem. Begins illegal settlements.

1987: First Intifada

Mass Palestinian uprising against occupation (20,000+ arrested, 1,000+ killed).

2023–2024: Gaza Genocide

After Hamas' Oct 7 attack, Israel kills 35,000+ Palestinians, bombs hospitals, blocks food/water.

Sources: UN Reports, B'Tselem, Amnesty International, Historical Records
