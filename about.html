About Us

Welcome to GO Connects, a free platform designed to help students discover educational opportunities. We connect users with third-party scholarship providers and academic services—all without collecting personal data.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify access to education by aggregating legitimate scholarships and academic resources. Note: We do not endorse or guarantee outcomes for linked third-party services.

We use Google AdSense to support our free service. Ads are provided by Google and may use cookies for personalized content. Learn how Google manages data.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Clearly label ads and disclose third-party relationships.
  • No Misleading Claims: We do not promise scholarship awards or guaranteed results.
  • User Privacy: We don’t collect data, but third-party ads may use cookies (opt-out here).

How It Works

  1. Browse: Explore our curated list of scholarships (we earn no commission).
  2. Redirect: Click links to official providers—we don’t control their content.
  3. Apply: Submit applications directly through third-party sites.

Note: GO Connects is not affiliated with scholarship providers. Always verify opportunities independently.

Why Choose Us?

We’re ad-supported to keep the platform free, but:

  • No paywalls or mandatory sign-ups.
  • No selling of user data (we don’t collect any).
  • Ads are clearly marked and comply with Google’s policies.
